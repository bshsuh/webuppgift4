Challange Description

    Beat Simon with x100 health multiplier.

    Extra Modifers

    • No play twice per turn Pictos (Faster Than Strong, Cheater)
    • No full break bar on death Picto (Breaking Death)
    • No dodging

    Guide Descrption

    Important details you need to know to understand this guide:

    • Monoco's third Gradient attack: your entier build and skill usage revolves around Monoco's stunning Gradient Attack
    • Speed attribute: your equipped pictos and stats should have speed as a priority
    • Skip phase 3 with Sciel's Burst damage: keep accumilating sun or moon charges and use it all when he is around half hp
    • Sufficient AP gain: No more AP gain from parrying, you need to rely on certain skills, Luminas and weapon passives to gain as much AP as possible (healing skills are great for this)
    • Gradient Charge gain: maximize the gained charge per turn with certain Luminas and spent AP
    • Once all the previous points are accounted for, start considering appropriate skills and Luminas to whittle Simon's hp down to 50%
